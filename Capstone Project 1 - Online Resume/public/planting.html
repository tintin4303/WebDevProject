Mangrove Planting Project

Address

  • BangPu Nature Education Center, Thailand

Date

  • Auguest 31, 2024 9:00AM

Paricipants

  • Fellow students from Assumption University

Initiating the Project

  • Inviting Participants

    Aside from the classmates who were there since the start, I contacted fellow students from the university who would like to participate in the activity.

  • Reaching out to the Center

    After I had gathered partipants, I contacted the center through Line App, but the center cannot communicate in English, so I asked for help from my Thai friend to translate. Then the center gave us a date and time (Aug 31, 2024 at 9:00AM).

Activities

  • Meet up

    We gathered near the University campus area around 6AM and took taxis to the center.

  • Arrival

    When we arrived, we met a group of young Thai people who were there for the same purpose. They collaborated with us and their translation made the process alot easier and saved time when communicating with the locals.

  • Planting

    After a long walk from the center through the forest to the coastal area, we planted mangrove trees around the area near the coast.
