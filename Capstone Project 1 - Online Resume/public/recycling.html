Recycling Project

About

  • This project is about our group of students at Assumption University carrying out demonstration and spreading awareness about recycling.

Address

  • Assumption University of Thailand, Suvarnabhumi Campus

Date

  • September 20, 2024 (First Meetup)
  • September 23, 2024 (Second Meetup)

Activities

  • First Meetup (Collecting recyclable trash around campus)

    We gathered at the campus sports area after class in the evening. Starting from the tennis courts, we collected recyclable materials mostly plastic bottles. After finishing up the area, we went on to have food together and called it a day.

  • Second Meetup (Demonstrating Recycling)

    Once again, we gathered but in a study room at a condo near the campus. This time we did DIY with the plastic bottles we collected the other day.
